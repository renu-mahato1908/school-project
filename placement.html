Councelling and Placement

Students graduating from Indus are guided to find admissions in prominent Institutions of higher learning both in India and overseas. Our IBDP/IBCP students have been admitted to the most reputed universities and colleges globally such as Stanford, Oxford, Brown, Cambridge, Harvard, Yale, Singapore University and numerous other prestigious colleges in the west and east, as well as in India.

Students who choose to study disciplines such as medicine, engineering, business studies, communication and arts have earned scholarships to various institutions and professional colleges through their academic and personal profiles. This has helped Indus earn credibility and a reputation of merit.
